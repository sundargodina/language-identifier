Language Classification

After data selection and preprocessing I used 22 languages from the original dataset Which Includes following Languages ⦁ English ⦁ Arabic ⦁ French ⦁ Hindi ⦁ Urdu ⦁ Portuguese ⦁ Persian ⦁ Pushto ⦁ Spanish ⦁ Korean ⦁ Tamil ⦁ Turkish ⦁ Estonian ⦁ Russian ⦁ Romanian ⦁ Chinese ⦁ Swedish ⦁ Latin ⦁ German ⦁ Dutch ⦁ Japanese ⦁ Thai

The model has an accuracy of 95%. It's a soft voting ensemble of Random Forest and Multinomial Naive Bayes.The dataset is relatively small, so it might not be that accurate :)
